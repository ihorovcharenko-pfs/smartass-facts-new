Animal Facts

Hundreds of surprising animal facts across cats, dogs, birds, fish, and more. Discover what's true and what's fake.

SmartAss Facts covers animal facts across every major vertebrate and invertebrate group, from apex predators to the insects that underpin most terrestrial ecosystems. Each animal category page collects the most surprising, verifiable claims about that species — trivia that tends to stick because it contradicts something we thought we knew. The facts in this collection are drawn from peer-reviewed zoology journals, field research, and the published work of academic naturalists. Everything is labeled ✓ TRUE or ✗ FAKE so you always know what's real.

The SmartAss Facts animal collection spans invertebrates, fish, amphibians, reptiles, birds, and mammals — covering both the familiar species that appear in popular culture and the less-known species whose biology is, if anything, more remarkable. Use the category links below to explore by animal group, or browse the fact cards for the most popular species.

Every animal fact in this collection links to its source — a peer-reviewed zoology study, a field research paper, or an official natural history publication — so you can verify any claim in seconds.
