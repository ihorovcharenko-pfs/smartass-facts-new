Common Questions

What is the difference between a meteor and a meteorite?

A meteor is the visible streak of light caused by a space rock burning in Earth's atmosphere. A meteorite is a piece of that rock that survives and reaches the ground. An asteroid is the rock while still in space.

What is the difference between venom and poison?

Venom is injected by biting or stinging (snakes, spiders). Poison harms when ingested, inhaled, or touched (poison dart frogs, many plants). An animal can be venomous, poisonous, or both — they're not synonyms.

What is the difference between weather and climate?

Weather is the short-term atmospheric state at a specific time and place — today's rain, this week's temperatures. Climate is the long-term average pattern of weather in a region over 30 or more years.
